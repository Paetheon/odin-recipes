Slow Cooker Pepper Steak

A great hearty meal that will definitely leave your tummy full and happy! Here's a recipe that one can do to make this delicious dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef sirloin, cut into 2 inch strips

  • 3/4 teaspoon garlic powder, or to taste

  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil

  • 1 cube beef bouillon

  • 1/4 cup of hot water

  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

  • 1/2 cup chopped onion

  • 2 large green bell peppers, roughly chopped

  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes, with liquid

  • 3 tablesppons soy sauce

  • 1 teaspoon white sugar

  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients

  2. Sprinkle beef sirloin strips with garlic powder. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat & sear beef strips, about 5 minutes per side. Transfer to a slow cooker.

  3. Mix boullion cube with hot water in separate container until issolved, then mix in cornstarch until dissolved.

  4. Pour into the slow cooker with beef strips. Stir in onion, green peppersm stewed tomatoes, soy sauce, sugar, & salt.

  5. Cover, and cook on High for 3 to 4 hours, or on Low for 6 to 8 hours.
