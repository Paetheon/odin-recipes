Matcha Coconut Smoothie

A healthy beverage that can easily be made at home that's great for many occasions. Here's a recipe that one can make easily at home!

Ingredients

  • 1 banana

  • 1 cup frozen mango chunks

  • 2 leaves kale, torn into several pieces

  • 3 tablespoons white beans, drained

  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened shredded coconut

  • 1/2 teaspoon matcha green tea powder

  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Combine banana, mango, kale, white beans, coconut, & matcha powder in a blender; add water. Blend mixture until very smooth.
